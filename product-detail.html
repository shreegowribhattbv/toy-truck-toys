Product Details

The Deluxe City Construction Truck is the perfect addition to any young builder's toy collection. This premium toy truck combines durability with interactive features to provide hours of imaginative play.

The truck features a fully functional dump bed that can be raised, lowered, and locked into position. The cab rotates 360 degrees and includes detailed interior elements like a steering wheel, seats, and dashboard.

With its realistic engine sounds activated by a push-button on the side, kids can enjoy an immersive construction site experience. The truck is constructed from high-quality die-cast metal and durable plastic components designed to withstand rough play.

This construction truck is compatible with most standard building block sets, allowing children to create elaborate construction scenarios. The truck's wheels roll smoothly on most surfaces, and the detailed treads provide excellent traction.
